Chicken Noodle Soup

A very simple chicken noodle soup recipe that is a very easy, very good substitute for when you don't have the time to make your soup from scratch!

This chicken noodle soup recipe can be completed in as little as 4o minutes. Whether you're feeling under the weather or you're just in need of a little comfort, this comforting soup will warm you up from the inside out. This soup goes great just by itself but if you want to add sides you can add sides like, biscuits, a grilled cheese sandwich, french baguettes, and more.

Ingedients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 4(14.5oz)cans chicken broth
  • 1(14.5oz)can vegetable broth
  • 1/2 pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teasponn dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste

How to make

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
